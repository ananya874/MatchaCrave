Product Description

Our Premium Ceremonial Matcha represents the pinnacle of Japanese tea craftsmanship. Sourced from the legendary Uji region, this matcha is made from the youngest, most tender leaves of shade-grown tea plants that have been carefully cultivated for over 400 years.

The leaves are stone-ground using traditional granite mills, a process that takes over an hour to produce just 30 grams of matcha. This slow, careful grinding preserves the delicate flavor compounds and vibrant green color that make our matcha exceptional.

Perfect for traditional tea ceremonies, this matcha offers a naturally sweet taste with deep umami notes and no bitterness. Rich in antioxidants, L-theanine, and natural caffeine, it provides sustained energy and mental clarity.

100% Organic Matcha Powder (Camellia sinensis)

Origin: Uji, Kyoto Prefecture, Japan

Processing: Stone-ground using traditional granite mills

Certifications: Organic, Non-GMO, Gluten-Free

Traditional Brewing Guide

You'll need: Matcha bowl (chawan), bamboo whisk (chasen), bamboo scoop (chashaku)

Step 1: Warm your bowl with hot water, then dry

Step 2: Sift 1-2 scoops (1-2g) of matcha into the bowl

Step 3: Add 60ml of hot water (70-80°C / 158-176°F)

Step 4: Whisk vigorously in a "W" motion until frothy

Step 5: Enjoy immediately while warm

Modern Method: Use a milk frother or small whisk for convenience
