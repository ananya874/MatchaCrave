Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between ceremonial and culinary grade matcha?
Ceremonial grade matcha is made from the youngest tea leaves and has a smoother, sweeter taste perfect for drinking. Culinary grade is great for cooking and baking.
How should I store my matcha?
Store matcha in a cool, dry place away from light and air. Once opened, keep it refrigerated and use within 2-3 months for best quality.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Free shipping on orders over $50 within Japan.
What's your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened products. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Can I visit your tea farm?
We offer guided tours of our partner tea farms in Uji during spring and fall. Contact us to schedule a visit and learn about matcha production.
